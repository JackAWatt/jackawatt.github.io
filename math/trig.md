# 6 trig functions

* Sin (sin)

$$sin(x) = \frac {opposite}{hypotenuse}$$

* Cosine (cos)

$$ cos(x) = \frac {adjacent}{hypotenuse}$$

* Tangent (tan)

$$ tan(x) = \frac {opposite}{adjacent}$$

* Secant (sec)

$$ sec(x) = \frac {hypotenuse}{adjacent}$$

* Cosecant (csc)

$$ csc(x) = \frac {hypotenuse}{opposite}$$

* Cotangent (cot)

$$ cot(x) = \frac {adjacent}{opposite}$$
