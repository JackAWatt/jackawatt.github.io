# Work, Power, and energy

Motion

**Work = Force * Distance**

Units: **Newton * meter (joule)**

**1 Nm = 1J**

**225 N * 1.75 m = 393.75 Nm = 393.75 J**


**1000 N * 0.8 m = 800 Nm = 800 J**

**Power = Work / Time**

**Power = Fd / t**

**Power = Force * Velocity**

Units: **Fd/s = Nm/s = J/s = watt**

**5000 N * 15 / 10 = 7500 watt**
