* 1: What is the resistance of a copper wire 400 ft long and 0.032′′ in diameter?

$$R = \frac {p l }{A}$$

$$R = \frac {p 400 }{A}$$

$$A_{CM} = (d_{mils})^2$$

$$A_{CM} = (0.032*1000)^2$$

$$A_{CM} = (32)^2$$

$$A_{CM} = 1024$$

$$R = \frac {p 400 }{1024}$$

$$R = \frac {10.37 400 }{1024}$$

$$R = 4.05078\ ohms$$
