$$56 = 0 + \frac {1}{2}(3.5)t^2$$

$$56 = \frac {1}{2}(3.5)t^2$$

$$56 = (1.75)t^2$$

$$\frac {56}{1.75} = t^2$$

$$32 = t^2$$

$$t = \sqrt{32}$$

$$t = 5.6568542495$$

$$t = 5.7 \text {seconds}$$
