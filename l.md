# 42

 A 1450 kg car accelerates from 3.20 m/s to 6.50 m/s. Calculate the amount of work done.

The amount of work done is 23207.25 J

{

KE = 1/2*mv^2

KE_i = 1/2 * 1450 kg * 3.20 m/s^2
KE_i = 7424 J

KE_f = 1/2 * 1450 kg * 6.50 m/s^2
KE_f = 30631.25 J

W = KE_f - KE_i
W = 30631.25 - 7424
W = 23207.25 J

}
